In Allison Road you will take on the role of the unnamed protagonist, who wakes up one day without any recollection of prior events. Over the course of five nights it is your objective to uncover the whereabouts of your family, unravel the mysteries of the house, and face off against Lily and other dark entities that are nested deep within its walls, while the clock is relentlessly ticking towards 3:00am.
What would you do if you could feel something stalking you in the dark in the safety of your own home? If you couldn't tell what's real and what's not?
The single-player campaign will mostly take place in one British townhouse.

Frequently Asked Questions

General questions

Are you planning a console release?

We are currently looking at both Playstation and Xbox to see what we can do about that. There’s nothing confirmed just yet, but we’ll keep you updated.

Will Allison Road have native VR support?

Allison Road is built with Unreal Engine 4, so it will have native VR support. Currently, however we are only testing it with an Oculus Rift DK2.

Is there a release date set?

We are currently aiming for end of Q3 2016.

Are you looking for Voice actors?

We are currently not looking for voice actors, but if things should change we’ll announce it here first thing.

Are you looking for Composers?

We are currently not looking for Composers, but if things should change we’ll announce it here first thing.

How big is your team?

We are currently 6 developers, one writer, one production manager, one business manager, and one marketing/PR consultant.
